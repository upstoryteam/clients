We believe we can help AutoTechIQ move more inspection reviews into authorized repairs.

What we see

Owners approve or defer repair spend after reviewing inspection evidence rather than phone-pressure upsells. Symptom intake, inspection review, and the approval moment are where shop-partner conversion lives.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make inspection evidence easy to act on

We'd start by guiding owners from reviewing inspection video and images to authorizing the work they understand.

Symptom intake → Inspection reviewed → Repair authorized
02

Let owners approve on their timeline

Worth testing how deferred and approved items are presented so owners commit when ready without feeling pressured.

03

Strengthen the shop-partner handoff

We'd look at how shops receive an authorized repair so partner conversion holds from review to booked work.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Inspection reviews reaching an authorized repair.
  • ·Intake-to-approval completion by shop partner.
  • ·Repeat authorizations from returning owners.
