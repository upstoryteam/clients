We believe we can help Beepi move more shoppers from a listing into a completed car purchase.

What we see

Buyers commit large payments before receiving the vehicle. The listing, inspection transparency, and checkout are where a shopper decides whether buying a car without a dealer visit feels safe.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make inspected listings build confidence

We'd start by bringing inspection detail and the guarantee into the listing so buyers feel ready to commit.

Listing view → Inspection reviewed → Purchase completed
02

Make checkout and delivery feel certain

Worth testing how delivery timing and the return window are presented so buyers complete a high-value purchase.

03

Make selling a car feel simple

We'd look at the seller flow so listing, inspection, and handoff convert sellers into completed sales.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Shoppers reaching a completed car purchase.
  • ·Listing-to-checkout completion.
  • ·Repeat buyer and seller activity after the first deal.
