We believe we can help Advocate move more eligible applicants to complete claim packets.

What we see

Disability claims require people to share medical, work, and life details while the outcome may take time. Advocate can make the first eligibility and document path feel supportive, specific, and worth continuing.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Show the claim path before document collection

We'd start by explaining eligibility, required records, case-manager support, and submission milestones before the full information ask.

Eligibility check → Claim path clear → Packet complete
02

Make missing records easier to gather

Worth testing a document checklist that separates applicant tasks, provider records, and case-manager follow-up.

03

Clarify status during long review periods

We'd look at status language that helps applicants understand submitted, pending, follow-up, and decision steps.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Eligible applicants that reach complete claim packets.
  • ·Document completion by claim type, condition category, and support path.
  • ·Applicant follow-through during case-manager and agency review steps.
