We believe we can help LaunchGood move more campaign visitors to completed first donations.

What we see

A donor needs to understand the campaign story, religious giving context, payment path, and impact before contributing. LaunchGood can make the first donation feel specific enough to become a repeat giving habit.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make the giving reason clear before checkout

We'd start by bringing campaign story, recipient, zakat or sadaqah context, and goal progress into one decision surface.

Campaign view → Giving reason clear → Donation completed
02

Connect one-time donations to daily giving

Worth testing a handoff from first donation into Daily Givers when the campaign category and donor intent match.

03

Clarify campaign updates after contribution

We'd look at post-donation updates that help donors see progress, share, or give again without needing a new campaign discovery moment.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Campaign visitors that complete a first donation.
  • ·Donation completion by campaign category, giving type, and payment method.
  • ·Repeat donation or Daily Givers activation after first contribution.
