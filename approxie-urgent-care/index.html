We believe we can help ApproXie move more patients from request to a completed urgent visit.

What we see

Patients commit symptoms, location, and a visit choice before care is delivered, while providers need fast routing. Request, booking, and the completed visit are where demand turns into care.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make booking a visit fast under pressure

We'd start by guiding patients from symptom and location entry to a booked same-day visit with minimal steps.

Request → Visit booked → Visit completed
02

Clarify in-person versus virtual choice

Worth testing how the choice between face-to-face and virtual is presented so patients pick the right care quickly.

03

Support providers as volume scales

We'd look at provider routing and follow-up so the experience holds as new markets and headcount come online.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Patient requests reaching a completed visit.
  • ·Booking completion by visit type.
  • ·Repeat use after the first urgent visit.
