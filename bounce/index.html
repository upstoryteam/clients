We believe we can help Bounce turn more location views into completed first bag-drop bookings.

What we see

Travelers choose Bounce when they are already carrying bags, watching the clock, and evaluating an unfamiliar drop-off location. The listing and checkout need to answer safety, timing, and pickup questions before payment.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Put drop-off confidence into the location card

We'd start by combining store type, hours, bag handling, reviews, and pickup instructions before the traveler opens checkout.

Location view → Drop-off clarity → Booking started
02

Make first booking recovery easier

Worth testing clearer alternatives when a location is closed, full, or too far once the traveler checks the map.

03

Tie rewards to the next travel use case

We'd look at post-pickup prompts that turn a successful first storage moment into airport, event, or next-city reuse.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Location views that become completed first bag-drop bookings.
  • ·Checkout completion by city, distance, and partner location type.
  • ·Repeat booking and reward activation after first successful pickup.
