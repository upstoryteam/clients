We believe we can help Repose move more families from planning into completed arrangements.

What we see

Families make irreversible funeral and cremation arrangements from home before services occur. Comparing providers, the arrangement, and committing are the compassionate moments where planning converts.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make planning lead to completed arrangements

We'd start by guiding families from comparing providers to completed, thoughtful arrangements.

Planning started → Provider compared → Arrangements made
02

Make pricing transparent and calm

Worth testing how real-time pricing is presented so families decide with clarity under emotional weight.

03

Keep the experience compassionate

We'd look at how the flow supports grieving families so they complete arrangements with care.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Families reaching completed arrangements.
  • ·Planning-to-arrangements completion.
  • ·Provider comparisons that lead to arrangements.
