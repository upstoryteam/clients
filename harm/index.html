What we see

Residents reach for city-approved support in life's hardest, most private moments. Finding the right resource and the first private connection are where the platform helps someone in crisis.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make finding support lead to a connection

We'd start by guiding residents from finding the right resource to a first private connection with support.

Resource found → Support matched → Connection made
02

Make the experience feel safe and private

Worth testing how privacy and safety are presented so residents reach out in hard moments.

03

Make city resources easy to navigate

We'd look at how the range of support is organized so residents find the right help quickly.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Residents reaching a first connection with support.
  • ·Resource-to-connection completion.
  • ·Return engagement with support over time.
