We believe we can help EzPag move more approved applicants to completed device pickup.

What we see

Applicants are making a payment commitment while traditional credit signals may not exist. The approval flow can make identity, terms, first payment, and pickup feel straightforward enough to finish.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Explain approval before the payment-plan ask

We'd start by showing what was approved, what is due today, and what happens at device pickup before collecting final consent.

Application → Approval explained → Device pickup
02

Clarify documents and identity recovery

Worth testing support paths for applicants who need another ID, proof, or store handoff to complete approval.

03

Connect first payment to account setup

We'd look at onboarding that helps customers understand reminders, payment method, and device status immediately after pickup.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Approved applicants that complete device pickup.
  • ·Application completion and approval recovery by document path.
  • ·First-payment readiness and early payment completion after pickup.
