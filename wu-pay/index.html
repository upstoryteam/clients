We believe we can help WU Pay move more shoppers from checkout into a completed cash payment.

What we see

Consumers shop online and pay cash through bill pay before receiving goods. The checkout choice and payment enrollment are where the alternative payment converts.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make checkout lead to a completed payment

We'd start by guiding shoppers from choosing WU Pay at checkout to a completed cash payment.

Checkout → WU Pay selected → Payment completed
02

Make the cash-payment option clear

Worth testing how the pay-cash option is presented so shoppers choose it confidently.

03

Smooth the payment-to-fulfillment handoff

We'd look at how the payment connects to fulfillment so shoppers feel confident their order is coming.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Shoppers reaching a completed payment.
  • ·Checkout-to-payment completion.
  • ·Repeat use after the first payment.
