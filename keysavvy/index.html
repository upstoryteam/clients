We believe we can help KeySavvy move more vehicle payment links to completed title-safe transactions.

What we see

Private-party car sales have a lot of anxiety packed into one payment moment. KeySavvy can make seller ownership, buyer funds, payoff, and title transfer feel like one coordinated closing path.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Show both sides the transaction status

We'd start by giving buyer and seller the same step language for ownership check, payment, payoff, and title transfer.

Payment link → Both sides ready → Transaction completed
02

Clarify liens and payoff without legal jargon

Worth testing plain-language status states for outstanding loans, payoff timing, and required seller action.

03

Package partner-specific transaction flows

We'd look at variants for marketplaces and credit unions that preserve the same buyer-seller confidence while fitting partner context.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Vehicle payment links that reach completed title-safe transactions.
  • ·Buyer and seller completion by listing source, lien status, and payment method.
  • ·Support or abandonment rates at ownership, payoff, and transfer milestones.
