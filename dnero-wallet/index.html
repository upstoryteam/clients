We believe we can help DNERO Wallet move more invited users from signup to a first sent transfer.

What we see

Invited users link a bank account, load funds, and send money before repeated proof of rewards lands. Invite acceptance, bank connection, the first transfer, and getting recipients through the claim link are the first-session moments.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make the first transfer easy to reach

We'd start by guiding invited users from accepting an invite through bank connection to a first sent transfer.

Invite accepted → Bank connected → First transfer
02

Make the claim link convert recipients

Worth testing the download-and-claim flow so recipients who are not yet on the app complete and join.

03

Make the rewards loop tangible

We'd look at how cash-back rewards are surfaced so users keep sending and inviting.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Invited users reaching a first sent transfer.
  • ·Invite-to-transfer completion.
  • ·Recipient claim-link conversion and repeat sending.
