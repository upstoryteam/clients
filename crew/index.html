We believe we can help Crew move more new users to a completed first ride or delivery.

What we see

Users book mobility or a delivery before service quality is proven. Signup, the first booking, and the completed trip or shipment are where new users decide whether the experience earns repeat use.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make the first booking easy to complete

We'd start by guiding new users from signup to a first completed ride or delivery.

Signup → First booking → Service completed
02

Make security cues clear

Worth testing how the security measures are presented so users feel confident booking a ride or shipment.

03

Encourage a second use

We'd look at what users see after a first ride or delivery so they return for another.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·New users reaching a first completed ride or delivery.
  • ·Signup-to-first-booking completion.
  • ·Repeat use after the first service.
