We believe we can help PatientWing move more study matches to qualified enrollment referrals.

What we see

Patients need to understand why a study may fit before they share health details or respond to site outreach. PatientWing can make eligibility, education, and referral feel like one patient-centered path.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Explain study fit before prescreening

We'd start by showing the plain-language reason a study may match, then ask for health details in smaller steps.

Study match → Fit explained → Prescreener completed
02

Make site outreach easier to anticipate

Worth testing a handoff that tells patients who may contact them, what to prepare, and when they should expect follow-up.

03

Create reusable flows by condition area

We'd look at enrollment patterns that adapt for rare disease, oncology, and sponsor-specific requirements without rebuilding each flow.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Study matches that reach qualified enrollment referrals.
  • ·Prescreener completion by condition area, channel, and study type.
  • ·Site handoff and referral response after patient qualification.
