## What's in this audit

Two opportunities tied to a single business outcome (more visitors through the funnel and more App Store reviews):

1. **The signup.** Their current signup gates on email before any product value is shown. We mock a "show first, ask second" version with an interactive demo of the AI Advocate working on a synthetic patient.
2. **The App Store ratings.** They have 9 ratings against an extremely positive review base. We include an interactive calculator showing how many ratings a well-timed in-app prompt could yield in 90 days.
