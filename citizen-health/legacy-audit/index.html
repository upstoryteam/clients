02 The App Store ratings

Increase App Store reviews by asking users at the right moment.

Your users use words like "REVOLUTIONARY" and "absolute life changer." Your App Store shows 9 ratings. The reviews are not there because nobody is being asked at the right time. Crossing roughly 100 ratings is where App Store search ranking begins to compound, so this is the kind of fix that pays back on every future news cycle.

Why this works
Per Apple's published benchmarks, in-app rating prompts convert at roughly 12 to 15 percent when fired right after a successful action, and under 2 percent when fired at random. The prompt itself is a few hundred lines of code. The hard part is choosing the moment, which the reviews above already tell us.
