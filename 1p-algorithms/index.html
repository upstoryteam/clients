We believe we can help 1P Algorithms move more new signups into active paid trading sessions.

What we see

Traders commit before any strategy has proven itself, so the first sessions carry real uncertainty. Account setup, strategy selection, and joining a first live or automated session are where confidence either builds or breaks.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make the first session reachable from signup

We'd start by guiding new traders from account setup through strategy selection to a first live session as one connected path.

Signup → Strategy selected → First session
02

Set expectations without overpromising returns

Worth testing plain-language framing of strategy risk and what a session includes, so traders choose with clear expectations rather than inflated hopes.

03

Clarify the rhythm after the first session

We'd look at what new traders see between sessions, so participation and renewal feel like a steady habit rather than a one-time try.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·New signups joining a first live or automated session.
  • ·Strategy selection and session activation by experience level.
  • ·Renewal and repeat participation after the first paid session.
