We believe we can help CareMates move more families from inquiry into started care services.

What we see

Older adults and their families choose companion care, planning, and wellness services before experiencing them. Inquiry, the match, and starting a service are where families commit to ongoing care.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make inquiry lead to started care

We'd start by guiding families from an inquiry through a match to a first started service.

Inquiry → Service matched → Service started
02

Make the services easy to understand

Worth testing how companion care, planning, and wellness are presented so families choose confidently.

03

Support ongoing engagement

We'd look at how a first service leads families to additional care and activities.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Families reaching a started service.
  • ·Inquiry-to-service completion.
  • ·Additional services after the first.
