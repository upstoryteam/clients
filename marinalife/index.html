What we see

Boaters book slips and pay fees before the on-water experience proves out, while marinas transact. Search, slip booking, and membership enrollment are where the two-sided ecosystem converts.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

Search → Slip selected → Slip booked
02

Make membership value clear

Worth testing how membership perks are presented so boaters enroll alongside booking.

03

Support marina transactions

We'd look at the marina side so slips are listed and bookings complete smoothly.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Boaters reaching a booked slip.
  • ·Search-to-booking completion.
  • ·Membership enrollment and repeat bookings over time.
