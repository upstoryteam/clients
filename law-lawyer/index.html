What we see

Users bring legal questions and commit to a path before outcomes are known. Asking a question, assigning a task, and moving toward a solution are where the platform proves its value.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

Question asked → Task assigned → Solution started
02

Make the ask-task-solve flow clear

Worth testing how the flow is presented so users understand how to move from question to resolution.

03

Keep users engaged to a solution

We'd look at how progress is shown so users stay through to a solved problem.

Ways to measure success

  • ·Question-to-task completion.
  • ·Tasks that progress to resolution.
