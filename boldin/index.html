We believe we can help Boldin move more planning starts to completed retirement scenarios.

What we see

People arrive with long-horizon money anxiety, then have to link income, taxes, expenses, and assumptions before the plan feels useful. The product can make the first scenario feel like progress before every financial detail is complete.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Build a first scenario before the full data ask

We'd start by helping users create a rough retirement picture, then improve accuracy as accounts and assumptions are added.

Planning start → First scenario → PlannerPlus prompt
02

Separate household confidence from advisor handoff

Worth testing a status view that shows what the user can decide alone and what is better saved for coaching or advisor review.

03

Match upgrade prompts to scenario depth

We'd look at plan prompts tied to clear planning milestones, such as tax view, drawdown analysis, or spouse collaboration.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Planning starts that reach a saved retirement scenario.
  • ·Scenario completion by channel: direct, employer, and advisor-led.
  • ·PlannerPlus conversion after users view drawdown, tax, or coaching prompts.
