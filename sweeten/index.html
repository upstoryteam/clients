We believe we can help Sweeten move more renovation intakes to contractor-ready project briefs.

What we see

Homeowners come with a high-budget renovation idea and a lot of uncertainty about scope, contractor fit, and timing. Sweeten can make project intake feel like useful preparation before any contractor match happens.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Guide homeowners to a contractor-ready brief

We'd start by structuring scope, budget, timeline, property type, and inspiration into a brief contractors can respond to.

Project idea → Brief ready → Contractor matched
02

Make bid comparison easier to understand

Worth testing bid-leveling support that highlights scope differences, assumptions, and payment options in plain language.

03

Support lean teams with reusable project patterns

We'd look at templates for kitchens, baths, whole homes, and commercial projects so guidance scales without extra manual lift.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Renovation intakes that reach contractor-ready project briefs.
  • ·Brief completion by project type, market, budget, and homeowner source.
  • ·Contractor response and matched-project progression after brief completion.
