We believe we can help Palatable move more diners from a dish view into a placed order.

What we see

Diners choose dishes and payment paths before meals are served, with real allergen stakes for health-sensitive users. The dish decision and the order flow are where a healthier dining choice converts.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make the dish decision lead to an order

We'd start by guiding diners from a dish view, with allergen and diet detail, to a placed order.

Dish viewed → Dish chosen → Order placed
02

Make allergen detail clear

Worth testing how allergen and nutrition information is presented so health-sensitive diners choose confidently.

03

Make healthier choices feel easy

We'd look at how the app guides diners toward dishes matching their diet.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Diners reaching a placed order.
  • ·Dish-view-to-order completion.
  • ·Repeat orders after the first.
