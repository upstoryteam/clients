The AI-native design team

Rick Russie

Founder, design lead

Is your product design team having trouble keeping up with the latest developments? Not sure if your current workflow is already out of date? Watching people adopt AI tools without a clear sense of what's worth changing?

In our work with product teams, we keep seeing the same thing: most design teams are overwhelmed by the speed of change and aren't sure what should change their workflow and what is just noise.

We built this workshop to help your team make sense of the current product design landscape and build more confidence in how you work together.

What you'll learn

Four practical modules for understanding where AI belongs in a modern product design workflow — for you and your team.

  1. 01
    Defining the landscape

    The AI product design tool landscape moves quickly. We'll map where the major tools fit and how teams are using them today, so you leave with a clear mental model for which tools do what.

  2. 02
    AI-native design systems

    Design systems are the bridge between your brand and AI output. We'll look at how tokens, components, and guidelines connect to the tools generating your UI, and how to keep that work on-brand without slowing your team down.

  3. 03
    Rapid prototyping in your design language

    Client briefs don't have to mean weeks of wireframes before something feels real. We'll walk through going from brief to credible live demo in your own design language, in minutes, without losing quality.

  4. 04
    Tokenomics: Controlling costs without sacrificing quality

    AI costs show up in subscriptions, API usage, and habits that are easy to miss. We'll cover where spend creeps in and what to do about it so you can control token and tool costs without sacrificing speed.
