We believe we can help Doola move more founder signups to completed business launch tasks.

What we see

Founders come to doola for a legal and financial launch sequence that can feel unfamiliar from outside the U.S. The dashboard can make formation, EIN, banking, and compliance feel like ordered progress instead of a bundle of admin products.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Turn formation checkout into a launch roadmap

We'd start by previewing state filing, EIN, banking, tax, and compliance milestones before founders pay.

Founder signup → Launch roadmap → Formation submitted
02

Clarify which tasks depend on prior approvals

Worth testing dependency labels so founders know why banking, taxes, or payments may wait on earlier steps.

03

Segment first tasks by founder location

We'd look at country-aware guidance for documents, expectations, and follow-up so international founders see relevant next steps first.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Founder signups that complete initial business launch tasks.
  • ·Formation submission and EIN handoff by country and entity type.
  • ·Dashboard task completion after payment and filing confirmation.
