We believe we can help Abby Care cut median days-to-certification to 45 in each new state.

What we see

Your new states are sending you applicants who already plan to care at home. The friction we see is training modules and Medicaid paperwork feeling like two different products. One guided queue would speed certification.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Merge certification and paperwork into one path

Caregivers bounce between training modules and Medicaid forms today. We'd start by showing one timeline with the next action always visible.

Apply online → Training + forms → Certified + paid
02

Treat the first documented shift as the proof moment

That shift is when paid caregiving feels concrete. We'd start by tightening the steps between approval and the first logged visit in the app.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Application started to certification complete within 45 days.
  • ·New state cohorts compared with Colorado matched by launch week.
  • ·First paid shift logged within 14 days of approval.
