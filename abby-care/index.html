We believe we can help Abby Care cut median days-to-certification to 45 in each new state.

Our read

Your new states are sending you applicants who already plan to care at home. The friction is the handoff between training modules and Medicaid paperwork feeling like two products. One guided queue would change how fast they certify. The opportunities below are where we would start.

Opportunities

Three places we would start in the product.

01

Merge certification and paperwork into one path

Caregivers bounce between training modules and Medicaid forms today. We would show one timeline with the next action always visible.

Apply online → Training + forms → Certified + paid
02

Treat the first documented shift as the proof moment

That shift is when paid caregiving feels concrete. We would tighten the steps between approval and the first logged visit in the app.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.
