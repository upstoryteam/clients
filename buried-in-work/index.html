We believe we can help Buried In Work move more new users into a started estate plan.

What we see

Families organize estates and end-of-life needs before a crisis forces rushed decisions. Enrollment, where users commit documents, beneficiaries, and plans, is a sensitive first step that has to feel supportive rather than overwhelming.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Make the first steps feel compassionate

We'd start by guiding new users through a first sensitive step so they begin organizing without feeling overwhelmed.

First visit → First step taken → Plan started
02

Break planning into manageable pieces

Worth testing how documents, beneficiaries, and plans are sequenced so users complete them over time.

03

Help families return to finish

We'd look at gentle re-engagement so users come back to complete a plan they have started.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·New users reaching a started estate plan.
  • ·First-visit-to-first-step completion.
  • ·Plans advanced over the following weeks.
