We believe we can help Bookend AI move more specialty-therapy cases to authorization-ready status.

What we see

Specialty pharmacy teams need confidence that the right therapy, documentation, and patient context are ready before authorization starts. A stronger workflow can make the upstream choice visible instead of burying it in admin work.

Opportunities to explore

Three places we'd start in the product.

01

Show the therapy decision before the auth packet

We'd start by making biosimilar choice, margin impact, and required documentation visible in one staff-facing review step.

Patient case → Therapy review → Auth packet ready
02

Make missing documentation easier to recover

Worth testing a case checklist that separates clinical gaps, insurance gaps, and patient-response gaps.

03

Create patient-touch moments for stalled cases

We'd look at lightweight patient messages that explain what is needed without exposing internal reimbursement complexity.

Ways to measure success

A few ideas for leading indicators we could track with you.

  • ·Specialty-therapy cases reaching authorization-ready status.
  • ·Time from case creation to complete clinical bundle by therapy area.
  • ·Denial and rework rates monitored after upstream review changes.
